Everything Flows

Everything Flows is an interactive P5.JS sketch. Waveforms, interference and flow inspired the work. This sketch is a departure from the Falling series. Rather than human bodies in free fall, particles are pixel bodies in motion. They fall and rise, settling to create patterns.
